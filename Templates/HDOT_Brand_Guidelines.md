# Hawaii Department of Transportation (HDOT) Brand Guidelines

This document outlines the official colors and typography used by the Hawaii Department of Transportation (HDOT), based on the State of Hawaii Web Style Guide.

## Typography

The standard typeface for all State of Hawaii agencies, including HDOT, is **Open Sans**.

*   **Primary Font:** Open Sans
*   **Fallback Fonts:** Helvetica Neue, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif
*   **Code/Technical:** Monospace

## Color Palettes

HDOT utilizes the State of Hawaii's nature-inspired themes. The **Makai** (Ocean) theme is most commonly associated with general transportation and maritime contexts, while **Mauka** (Mountain) may be used for inland projects.

## Technical Standards (CADD)

For technical drawings and construction plans, the Highways Division follows the **CADD Drawing Standards (Chapter 6)**:
*   **Fonts:** Standard CADD fonts (e.g., Romans, Simplex)
*   **Line Weights & Colors:** Defined by specific pen tables in the HDOT Drafting Manual.
